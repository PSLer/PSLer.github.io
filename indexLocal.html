Dr. Junpeng Wang

Post-doc Computer Graphics & Visualization Technical University of Munich Email: junpeng.wang@tum.de Address: Boltzmannstr. 03, 85748 Garching b. Munich, Germany

I'm a Postdoc (since June 2023) in the group Computer Graphics & Visualization chaired by Prof. Westermann. Beforehand, I was a PhD candidate in the same place (11.2018 ~ 06.2023). My research interests include (i) lightweight structural design and optimization with a special focus on geometry-based approaches and (ii) scientific visualization, typically on tensor and mesh data.

Tools & Software

➤ TOP3D_XL - A pure MATLAB implementation for high-resolution topology optimization and porous infill optimization in 3D.
➤ SGLDBench - A high-performance simulation suite for stiffness-targeted lightweight design using different strategies, covering topology optimization, porous infill optimization, stress lines-guided infill, graded Voronoi infill, conforming lattices, and volumetric Michell trusses. Different strategies can be consistently evaluated and compared. Simulating scale can be up to 400 million structural DOFs on a PC equipped with 64 RAM.
➤ 3D-TSV - Trajectory-based stress tensor visualization in 3D.
