I'm a Post-doc researcher (since 06.2023) in the group Computer Graphics & Visualization chaired by Prof. Westermann at the TUM. Beforehand, I obtained my PhD with the highest distinction (Summa Cum Laude) from the same place in 2023.

My research is focused on lightweight structural design and optimization, spanning large-scale numerical simulation, design for manufacturing, geometric modeling, stress tensor visualization, CAE software development, etc.
